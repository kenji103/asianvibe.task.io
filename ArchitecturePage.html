The Beauty of Asian Architecture



Architecture in Asia reflects the continent’s rich cultural diversity, long history, and varied climates. Traditional Asian architecture often emphasizes harmony with nature, balance, and symbolism. For example, temples, palaces, and houses are frequently designed with courtyards, wooden structures, and detailed carvings that represent spiritual or cultural beliefs.
