Various delicious drinks




Drinks in Asia are very diverse and reflect the cultures of the region. In many countries, tea is the most popular drink. For example, green tea is widely consumed in China and Japan. In Southeast Asia, people often enjoy sweet drinks like Thai iced tea or fresh coconut water. Bubble tea, which started in Taiwan, has also become very popular around the world. These drinks are not only refreshing but also an important part of social life in many Asian cultures.


Traditional Tea Culture in Asia

Traditional tea culture is an important part of daily life and hospitality in countries like China and Japan.

Sweet and Iced Drinks of Southeast Asia

Sweet and iced drinks are very popular in Southeast Asia, especially in hot climates where refreshing beverages are part of daily life.

Modern Popular Drinks (Bubble Tea and New Trends)

Modern popular drinks like Bubble Tea show new trends in Asia, combining traditional flavors with creative and modern styles.
